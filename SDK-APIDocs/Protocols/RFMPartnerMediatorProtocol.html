RFMPartnerMediatorProtocol Protocol Reference

Conforms to NSObject
Declared in RFMPartnerMediator.h

Overview

All partner mediator subclasses must conform to RFMPartnerMediatorProtocol

Two methods, requestAdWithSize: cancelRequest, and must be subclassed in order for the SDK to properly pass control to a partner mediator and maintain proper user experience.
