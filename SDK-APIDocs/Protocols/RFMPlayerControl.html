RFMPlayerControl Protocol Reference

Conforms to NSObject
Declared in RFMPlayerControl.h

Overview

This protocol defines the methods that control the playback of video. The video ad session utilizes this protocol to initiate ad playback, resume content, get duration, and to check for playback errors.
