RFMPartnerInterstitialDelegate Protocol Reference

Conforms to RFMPartnerMediatorDelegate
Declared in RFMPartnerMediator.h

Overview

Interstitial mediator subclasses must conform to RFMPartnerInterstitialDelegate

These delegate methods allow the mediator to communicate back to the SDK to inform the publisher when certain events have or will occurred. Even though these delegate methods are optional, interstitial mediator subclasses must implement them.
