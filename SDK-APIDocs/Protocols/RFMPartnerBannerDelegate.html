RFMPartnerBannerDelegate Protocol Reference

Conforms to RFMPartnerMediatorDelegate
Declared in RFMPartnerMediator.h

Overview

Banner mediator subclasses must conform to RFMPartnerBannerDelegate

These delegate methods allow the mediator to communicate back to the SDK to inform the publisher when certain events have or will occurred. Even though these delegate methods are optional, banner mediator subclasses must implement them.

– willPresentFullScreenModal

Optional Delegate callback to inform the SDK that the banner mediation will present modal fullscreen.
